about me

I am a graduate student in data science at Duke University. Previously I went to National University of Singapore and EPFL.

Besides data science and data storytelling, I also like contemporary art and travelling.

Having lived in China, Singapore, Switzerland & United States, I understand English, Chinese and French.

I have given talks and workshops such as Introduction To Visualization for Women Who Code and Drawing with Data Workshop at Airbnb APAC.
